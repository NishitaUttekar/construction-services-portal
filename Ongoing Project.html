Emerald Heights

Emerald Heights is a premium residential project located in the heart of Pune City, offering a perfect blend of modern architecture and comfortable living. This high-rise apartment complex features luxurious 2BHK, 3BHK, and penthouse units, designed to provide spacious and well-ventilated homes. The project, spread across 10 acres, consists of 5 towers with 20 floors each, offering stunning views of the city skyline. Currently under construction, Emerald Heights is expected to be completed by December 2026.

The project boasts state-of-the-art amenities, including a clubhouse, swimming pool, fully equipped gym, landscaped gardens, jogging tracks, a dedicated children's play area, and a multi-purpose sports court. A 24/7 security system with CCTV surveillance, intercom facilities, and smart access control ensures the safety of residents. Sustainability is a key focus, with rainwater harvesting, solar-powered lighting in common areas, and an efficient waste management system integrated into the project.

Emerald Heights offers flexible pricing and payment plans, with unit prices starting at ₹75 lakh for 2BHK and ₹1.2 crore for 3BHK. Buyers can benefit from easy home loan assistance and attractive EMI options. Limited-time early-bird discounts are available for pre-launch bookings.

Strategically located near NH-48, the project provides seamless connectivity to major business hubs, educational institutions, hospitals, and entertainment zones. Schools like Springfield International, hospitals such as City Care Medical, and shopping malls like Greenview Plaza are just a few minutes away. The upcoming metro station, located 1 km from the site, further enhances accessibility. The construction progress is updated regularly on our website, with the latest images and site visit details. The project is RERA-approved (RERA ID: ABC1234567), ensuring complete transparency and legal compliance.

Skyline Corporate Tower

Project Overview
Corporate Tower is a state-of-the-art commercial office building under construction in the heart of Nagpur. This 25-story high-rise spans over 500,000 square feet and is designed to accommodate premium office spaces for multinational corporations, IT firms, and financial institutions. Strategically located near major business hubs and public transport networks, the project aims to redefine the city’s corporate landscape with cutting-edge architecture and world-class amenities.

Key Features
Designed by Innovate Architects, Skyline Corporate Tower boasts a sleek glass façade, energy-efficient design, and open-concept office spaces. The building features high-speed smart elevators, automated climate control, and a centralized security system to ensure a seamless workplace experience. Sustainability is a core focus, with solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and green terraces integrated into the design to achieve a LEED Gold Certification. Tenants will also have access to luxurious conference rooms, a business lounge, a fully-equipped fitness center, and rooftop dining with panoramic city views.

Construction Status
The project is currently in the structural completion phase, with the external framework 80% finished. Interior work, including electrical and plumbing installations, is in progress, and glass panel installations for the façade have begun. Construction began in Q2 2023, and the expected completion date is December 2025. Regular progress updates are provided, ensuring transparency and adherence to timelines.

Investment & Leasing Opportunities
Skyline Corporate Tower offers flexible office spaces ranging from 2,000 sq. ft. to full-floor layouts to accommodate businesses of all sizes. The leasing rate starts at $35 per square foot, with attractive pre-leasing options available for early investors. With over 80% of the office spaces already booked, potential tenants are encouraged to secure their space soon. The project is particularly ideal for technology firms, law offices, and financial enterprises looking for a prestigious business address.

Safety & Compliance
The tower is constructed following international building codes and seismic safety standards, making it highly resistant to earthquakes and other structural threats. 24/7 surveillance, biometric access controls, and fire suppression systems ensure the highest levels of safety for occupants. Emergency evacuation plans and disaster response measures have been meticulously designed to comply with all regulatory requirements.

Green Haven Residences

The Green Haven Residences an ongoing eco-friendly residential housing project designed to promote sustainable living. Located in Nashik, this development integrates modern architectural designs with environmentally responsible features. The project incorporates solar panels, energy-efficient lighting, and smart home technology to reduce energy consumption. Rainwater harvesting, greywater recycling, and water-efficient fixtures ensure water conservation, while recycled and locally sourced materials minimize environmental impact. The community will feature rooftop gardens, green spaces, and waste management systems to promote a healthier lifestyle. Designed for maximum natural light and ventilation, the homes maintain a low carbon footprint while offering contemporary comfort. Currently under construction, the project is progressing in phases, with foundation and structural work completed, roofing and solar panel installation ongoing, and interior work scheduled next. Future residents will enjoy lower utility bills, improved air quality, and a sustainable community environment. Bookings are open, and site visits are available for those interested in experiencing this innovative, green living space.
