Who We Are?

We are one of the Best Managed Companies in Pune with a strong track record for planning and executing challenging projects on time everytime.

NISA Construction Company was established in 1992 by Sayali Kakade and Nishita Uttekar with a focus on executing linear infrastructure and industrial construction projects with ease. We are proud to be known as the pioneers of Tremix Flooring in Pune and Maharashtra. Our expertise and unwavering commitment to revolutionizing the domain of flooring and pavements have enabled us to execute Tremix flooring and industrial construction projects smoothly and efficiently since our inception.
Our organization boasts a perfect blend of experience and innovation, which ensures seamless planning and execution of even the most complex and challenging projects. We are dedicated to meeting the diverse needs of our clients and exceeding their expectations through our high standards of quality in engineering and project delivery. We strive each day to improve our services and maintain our reputation as industry leaders.
